Foxy

Although Foxy has appeared in all the games so far, he's a little different from the other core animatronics because he has his own stage called the Pirate Cove and he looks and talks like a pirate. He's also out of order, with visible damage to his body, and his jaws with its rows of sharp teeth hangs loose. He doesn't join in on Freddy Fazbear's Pizza theme song, and Freddy sings the lines introducing Foxy.

Novel Appearances

The Silver Eyes, The Twisted Ones, The Fourth Closet

Short Story Collection Appearances

Lonely Freddy, Step Closer, The New Kid, What We Found, Sea Bonnies, and Stichwraith Stingers.
