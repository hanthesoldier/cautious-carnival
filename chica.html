Chica

Chica is a Animatronic Chicken that loves to eat! Well, thats her personality according to Freddy Fazbear's Pizza theme song, where she sings Keep an eye on your pizza, cause I'm a bird who likes to eat! She also has Let's Eat on her bib to make it extra clear. But she's an animatronic chicken, so how can she?
