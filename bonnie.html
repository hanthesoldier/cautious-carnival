Bonnie

Bonnie is a bunny a human-sized animatronic bunny who plays in Freddy Fazbear's band and carries a red Flying V guitar. His personality may seem laid-back, but like a lot of the animatronics, how he talks and how he acts aren't the same thing. In fact, Bonnie is often the most active and the most aggressive one out of the animatronics. In Five Nigths at Freddys, he's usually the first one to start moving in the direction of the office each night.
