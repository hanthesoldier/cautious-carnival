Freddy Fazbear

Freddy Fazbear is the mascot of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. According to the lyrics of his song, Freddy is a "real fun-loving guy". He's a cheerful and friendly animatronic bear who loves to have fun and entertain children during the day!
